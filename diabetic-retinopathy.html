AI-Based Diabetic Retinopathy Detection

Developed a deep learning system to detect diabetic retinopathy from retinal images, supporting both binary (DR vs No DR) and multi-class classification.

Tools & Technologies

  • Python
  • TensorFlow
  • ResNet50, EfficientNet, DenseNet
  • Kaggle Dataset

Key Results

  • 95.2% accuracy
  • F1-score: 0.95 | AUC-ROC: 0.98
  • Improved multi-class accuracy from 91% → 95.6%

Business Impact

Designed for early screening of diabetic retinopathy with high recall, reducing false negatives and improving clinical reliability.
